TIMELINE OF TERROR

OCTOBER 2025 - PRESENT
Israel's war on Gaza in 2025 has killed at least 54,510 Palestinians and injured 124,901 since October 7, 2023. In just the past week, hundreds have been killed, including 43 in a single airstrike targeting aid seekers.

OCTOBER 2023
Israeli forces launch brutal assault on Gaza, killing thousands of civilians including women and children in airstrikes and ground attacks.
MAY 2021
11-day Israeli aggression on Gaza kills 256 Palestinians including 66 children, wounds over 1,900 people, and destroys thousands of homes and buildings.
2018-2019
Great March of Return protests see Israeli snipers kill 223 Palestinians and wound over 8,000, including journalists, medics, and children.
